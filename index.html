About Us

SpeakEasyDoc implements and integrates Open Whisper System's Signal end-to-end encryption protocol with Hyperpad to provide maximum privacy and security. Once the user has successfully registered, they can open a document, add collaborators by username or phone number, and simply start typing.

Why SpeakEasyDoc?

According to a report from Risk Based Security, 2016 broke the previous all-time high for the number of records exposed from reported data breaches. This along with the revelations that governments around the world are actively engaging in mass surveillance raises important questions and concerns on how to best exchange information and ideas and communicate privately.

We are building a real-time text editor with end-to-end encryption (E2EE) for team collaboration using Hyperpad, a peer-to-peer live text-editor, and Open Whisper System’s E2EE Signal Protocol. Signal combines the Double Ratchet Algorithm, prekeys, and a triple Diffie–Hellman (3-DH) handshake, and uses Curve25519, AES-256 and HMAC-SHA256 as primitives.
